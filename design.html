New York Islanders Season Campaign

This was a personal project, imagining the season campaign for the NHL's New York Islanders for the 2022-2023 season.

Came up with "UNYIELDING" which includes "NYI", also an acronym for the New York Islanders. Labeled color usage and font type.

An example of a set of gameday graphics, showcasing before-the-game info, and in between periods scores. These came in home, away, third jersey and retro jersey variants.

An example of a social media graphic showcasing a player's birthday. Note the orange bars that break into fourths, a recurring theme throughout.

A unique graphic celebrating a player's milestone, in this case, scoring 300 career goals. The orange bars often carry information, the date the milestone occured, for example.

Another milestone graphic, showcasing a player's first NHL goal. Another common theme throughout is blue paint being brushed messily across the graphic to create divide, with hints of orange and white splatter dots in the background.

A graphic showing off the Islander's schedule for the upcoming year, including a legend to denote which games are at home, away, or wearing their third or retro jerseys.

A graphic revealing a long awaited jersey for Islander fans. Note the blue paint creating a background, the jersey "jumping" off the page, and the white and orange lines carrying information and showcasing the design.

A tribute for a well-beloved player who is leaving the team via trade. The orange bars that break into fourths is also lifted from the Islander's logo, which has four bars on the hockey stick, each signifying a Stanley Cup win the club has won.

Non-profits

Various Non-profits that I've worked with.

On top, the pre-supplied logo of Aevidum, a student-created and led suicide awareness group. Aevidum means "I have your back" and was started after a fellow student commited suicide. They are starting a subgroup called "Friend 2 Friend" where students counsel each other. After some back and forth with ideas, the bottom logo was selected.

The ACC is a group that helps relocated refugees from Africa who come over to America looking for a new way of life. This logo is re-supplied and I did not help with it's involvement.

They are looking for a logo for their second season of their podcast, and asked that I came up with six mockup ideas to use for their podcast. I studied what their goals are, and sketched about ten rough ideas. From that, trimmed it down to six and submitted it for approval to see where their direction lays.

While this logo made the final two options and was the choice that I was pushing, it ultimately fell to the side. It's a simple roundel with a man wearing headphones, on a canvas of the colors of the ACC, making the connection.

After selecting the logo with the microphone coming up out of the continent, I offered to send a few color schemes so they could see what would look best.

The final lockup version of the logo. This was submitted in .jpg, .png, .ai and .psd files for their usage, which is still being used to this day.

The Latine Economic Developmental Center came to me and asked for a logo to celebrate their upcoming 30th anniversary. Using this logo, I was tasked to come up with a new logo that was on brand and celebrated what they've accomplished. please note, this logo was not done by me and I did not help in it's creation.

I came up with several ideas and sent them to my connection at the company. This project also is the subject of repeat business. My connection at the LEDC previously worked at the above listed ACC, and was so happy with how that came out they sought me out again.

After selecting a general look, we decided to gold-plate it, and I offered up a few final touches to show them what the last stages could look like.

After a last bit of discussion, we finalized this logo. It's gold sheen celebrates the company's 30th anniversary, and uses the LEDC's main circle logo as the zero.
